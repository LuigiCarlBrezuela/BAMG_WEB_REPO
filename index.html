About Us

At Lola Feling RestoBar, we're passionate about sharing the rich flavors and traditions of Filipino cuisine with our community. Our mission is to create a unique and memorable dining experience that showcases the beauty of Filipino culture.

  • Lola Feling RestoBar was founded by a team of Filipino food enthusiasts who wanted to bring a taste of their homeland to the city.
  • Our chefs are trained in the traditional techniques of Filipino cooking, using only the freshest ingredients to create authentic dishes.
  • Our restaurant is designed to evoke the warm and welcoming atmosphere of a Filipino home, with comfortable seating and vibrant decor.

At Lola Feling RestoBar, we're committed to providing exceptional service and creating a memorable dining experience for every guest. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply looking for a delicious meal, we invite you to join us and taste the magic of Filipino cuisine.

A Taste of the Philippines

Indulge in our mouth-watering Chicken Inasal, a classic Filipino dish that's sure to transport your taste buds to the Philippines. Our chicken is marinated in a blend of spices and herbs, then grilled to perfection to bring out its natural flavors. The result is a tender and juicy chicken that's smothered in a sweet and savory sauce, making every bite a delight.

A Crispy Pork Leg

Crispy Pata is a popular Filipino dish that originated from the Spanish-influenced cuisine of the Philippines. It is a type of braised pork leg that is slow-cooked in a mixture of spices, vinegar, and soy sauce until the meat is tender and the skin is crispy.

Taste the Four Season

A harmonious blend of flavors and textures, our Four Seasons Chicken dish features a medley of chicken cooked in four distinct sauces, each representing a season: a light and refreshing coconut milk-based sauce (Spring), a spicy and savory tomato-based sauce (Summer), a rich and nutty peanut sauce (Autumn), and a warm and comforting adobo-based sauce (Winter).

Tropical Beef Delight

Savor the rich flavors of our grilled beef, smothered in a sweet and tangy mango sauce, infused with the warmth of the Philippines. This dish is a masterful blend of savory beef and fresh mango, creating a truly unforgettable culinary experience that will transport your taste buds to the tropical islands of the Philippines..

Fall-Off-The-Bone Baby Back Ribs with Fresh Summer Salad

Indulge in our tender and juicy baby back ribs, slow-cooked to perfection and slathered in a rich and tangy BBQ sauce. Paired with a refreshing side of fresh summer salad, featuring crisp cucumber, crunchy lettuce, and sweet corn, this dish is a perfect blend of smoky and savory flavors. The fall-off-the-bone tender ribs are sure to delight, while the light and refreshing salad provides a delightful contrast. A must-try at Lola Feling RestoBar!
